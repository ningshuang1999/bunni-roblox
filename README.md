We understand that managing dozens of accounts or trying to open multiple Roblox windows at the same time can sometimes become quite complicated. Bunni was created to transform those complex processes into something simpler, more convenient, and more secure—allowing you to dedicate your full time to enjoying your favorite games.

---

## Features

### 🌿 Account Management

Instead of having to remember passwords or struggle with constantly switching accounts through too many steps, Bunni helps you gather everything in one place. You can set custom nicknames for each account and assign color-coded tags such as "Main Account", "Friend Account", or "Alt" for easy identification. Everything is neatly organized—just one glance, and you see it all.

### 🎮 Your Personal Game Library

Don’t let your favorite titles fade into oblivion. You can create your own custom game lists, name them however you like, and let Bunni automatically update the latest information from Roblox. Searching for and launching a game has never been this fast.

### 🚀 Convenience

Bunni supports running multiple Roblox windows at the same time. Want to “play” multiple accounts simultaneously? Or bring your alt accounts into a friend’s server together? The Multi Play feature and modes such as Normal, Follow, and Friend allow you to do it all with just a single click.

### 🔒 Privacy Commitment

Bunni is designed with one principle in mind: Your data belongs to you, and only you.

Local Storage: All login information stays securely on your computer.

No “curiosity”: Absolutely no data is transmitted to external servers. Bunni simply acts as a tool to process actions that provide convenience for you—it does not collect any data whatsoever.
